  • Oldham County sits on the banks of the Ohio River. Enjoy fishing, swimming and cruising in a boat, among many other things to pass the time by the water.

  • Train lovers from all around the country come to see the train tracks run right down Main Street in the middle of La Grange.

  • Kentucky is world known for our Derby's, so come on out for a drive down the "Scenic Route" to admire the beautiful horses.
