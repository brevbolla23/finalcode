    • Great Scenery

    Oldham County is in northern Kentucky on the banks of the mighty Ohio River.
    A short boat ride across the river will take you to Indiana where you can enjoy more fishing and hiking.
    There are plenty of parks and hikes that are available in the area, as well as an aquatic center, our public pool.

  • Falling Rock Park

    • Falling Rock Quarry is a favorite among residents of Oldham County and surrounding areas.
    Lush greenery is the backdrop to a blue and teal quarry waters.
    Make sure to take a floating device (mandatory) and float amongst friends.

  • Yell Dell Botanical Gardens

    • Yew Dell Botanical Gardens is an internationally recongnized center for education, plants, and gardening.
    As well as being a nonprofit facitlity, they give guided tours and educational classes and it's a great venue for weddings and celebrations.
    Mosy around the beautiful landscape and take in all the clean air. Make sure to stop and smell the roses.

  • 3rd Turn Oldham Gardens

    • Would you like to drink beer at a huge table, under a row of trees and foliage?
    3rd Turn Oldham Gardens has got you covered, literally.
    The second location for 3rd Turn Brewing (the first location being in J-Town, KY) offers many differents types of beer and meades.
    Over an acre to strech you legs and roam the gardens, they aslo have summer concert series that are not to be missed.
