  • Savor Mex

    • Want some authentic flavors from south of the border? Look no further than Savor Mex. A family-ran quick eatery is the perfect place to grab some tacos, sopas, or tomales and be on your way. With the selection of fresh juices and salsas, there is no wonder why this is some of the best Mexican cuisine in town!

  • Raw Sushi Bar and Grill

    • Have your sushi made by an award-winning chef! Korean and Japanese dishes are the specialties at this restaurant. Amazing bulbogi and bimbimbap will take you to Seoul, South Korea. Your taste buds travel to Japan with soothing Udon noodles and fresh, colorful sushi.
